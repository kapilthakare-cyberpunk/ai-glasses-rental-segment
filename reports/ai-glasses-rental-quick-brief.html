AI Glasses Rental - Quick Brief

Should Primes & Zooms launch AI glasses rental in Pune?

A concise summary of the AI glasses rental opportunity for the Pune market. Covers product overview, pricing strategy, and a recommended go/no-go decision with phased rollout plan.

Executive Summary

AI-powered smart glasses (Ray-Ban Meta Gen 2) represent a viable new rental segment for Primes & Zooms. The Pune market has zero direct competitors, strong social media demand, and rental yields of Rs.500–900/day (1.2–2.0% of retail MRP). We recommend launching with 2× Ray-Ban Meta Wayfarer Gen 2 for Rs.80K–Rs.92K total investment, testing demand over 30–60 days, and scaling only if utilization exceeds 40%. This is a low-risk, high-upside category entry.

Key Points

  • Zero competition in Pune - no operator currently offers AI glasses rental
  • Strong demand signal - Ray-Ban Meta Gen 2 trending on Instagram (2.1M+ posts), Amazon India search volume rising 300%+ YoY
  • Healthy rental yield - Rs.500–900/day vs Rs.80K–Rs.52K purchase cost (25–35 rentals to break even)
  • Start small, validate - 2× Gen 2 units, Rs.80K–Rs.92K total, 30–60 day test period
  • Phased expansion - add Oakley HSTN + Vanguard in Phase 2 if demand confirmed

Recommendation

Suggested Action
GO - Launch with 2× Wayfarer Gen 2 (Clear lens) immediately. Test demand for 30–60 days. If utilization >40%, expand to 4–6 units including Oakley models. Security deposit = 100% retail value. Mandatory insurance required.
